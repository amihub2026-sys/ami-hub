Account Deletion – AMI HUB

At AMI HUB, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can do so using the methods below.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  • Open the AMI HUB app
  • Go to Profile
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm your request

Option 2: Request via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us:

Email: amihub2026@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please include your registered email or phone number and your name.

What Happens After Request

  • Your account will be deleted within 7 days
  • You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information: name, email, phone number
  • Listings, posts, and uploaded content
  • Account preferences

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as transaction or payment records and legal compliance data.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all your data and listings
