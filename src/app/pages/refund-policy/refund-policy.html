Refund Policy

Clear and transparent policy for all AmiHub services

Introduction

At AmiHub.in, we believe in delivering a clear, straightforward experience when using our services. To ensure transparency and avoid misunderstandings, we have implemented the following No Refund Policy for all package purchases.

No Refund Policy

  • Once a package has been purchased, it is non-refundable.
  • This applies to all advertising packages, regardless of duration or pricing.

Why No Refunds?

Service Activation

Once a package is purchased, ads are immediately activated and visible to buyers. The service begins instantly.

Time-Limited Ads

All packages have expiry durations. Once activated, the time starts counting, making refunds not feasible.

Before You Buy

We recommend reviewing:

  • Features offered
  • Ad visibility duration
  • Pricing structure
  • Expiry time

This helps you choose the right package.

Package Modifications

Purchased packages cannot be modified or transferred. To extend visibility, a new package must be purchased.

Customer Support

If you face any technical issues, our support team is available to help. While refunds are not provided, we ensure quick resolution.

Dispute Resolution

In rare cases where services are not delivered as promised, we will investigate and provide a suitable solution such as:

  • Fixing technical issues
  • Extending ad duration

Final Note

We recommend contacting AmiHub support before making a purchase if you have any questions.

Thank you for choosing AmiHub. We are committed to supporting your buying and selling experience!
